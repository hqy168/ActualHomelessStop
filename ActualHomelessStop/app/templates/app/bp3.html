Homeless Stop is back at Feed My Starving Children!

Learn more about our active participation in the fight for those less fortunate.

Follow us as we work to stop homelessness and help those in need, one step at a time

On October 10th, Homeless Stop partnered with The 8 Foundation to pack meals for those less fortunate at Feed My Starving Children. Together, we packed over 60 boxes and, together with the rest of the session, fed 148 kids 3 meals a day for a whole year.


A big thank you to everyone who participated!


Every four seconds, a person dies of hunger. Starvation malnutrition remains a worldwide problem impacting billions of people aroung the globe. You can make an impact by volunteering at organizations such as Feed My Starving Children or by donating to them.
